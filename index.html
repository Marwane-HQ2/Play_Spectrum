Play Spectrum !

Spectrum: a game concept...

How do you feel when colors match perfectly on a beautiful gradient ? In this this game you must recreate a gradient by moving tiles until everything looks great ! Follow your inner voice and you will build a masterpiece !

You need to move the color tiles to recreate the spectrum, you can move the tiles by clicking on the first tile you want to move and the tile to exchange spot with. On this version, tiles move only in their own column ! and once you are done a message should appear if you completed this puzzle !

-
You have only 8 seconds to memorize the pattern and the countdown started ! Here is some help for you:
The top-left color is #FF0000
The top-right color is #0000FF
The bottom-left color is #00FF00
The bottom-right color is #000000
